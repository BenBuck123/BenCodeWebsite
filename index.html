Benjamin Buck

My name is Ben Buck and I am 18 years old. Ever since I was a junior in highschool, I have had a fascination with graphics and web design in specific. I used to only design websites in photoshop for my designing eye, but then when I realized how much fun I had figuring out how to actually code functionality into the websites, I decided to become a website developer for a living. Designing websites is a passion of mine and I love everything about it. I love seeing the stuff I make come together in a functional website that people will then be able to navigate. I was born and raised in Canada and still reside there today. I plan on traveling the world, designing websites on my travels, and I plan to expand my business in the web industry in the future.
